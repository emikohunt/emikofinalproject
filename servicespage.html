Here's what I can help you with

Copy Writing and Editing

I offer services writing content from scratch, or editing pre-existing content. With a graduate diploma in Writing and Editing, I have a strong command of words and experience writing for web, writing with good SEO practices, and simplifying copy so it is easy to understand.

Digital Strategy Consulting

Whether you're a start up company or are an established business, I can help develop and guide your digital strategy. This involves an initial meet up to get to know your business and your business goals. From there we can develop a strategy that uses the best digital marketing channels for your business. I can also provide training so that you understand best practice and how to engage with these channels on an ongoing basis.

Brand Consulting

Whether you're a start up company or are an established business, I can help develop and guide your brand. From aesthic look and design to language and alignment. I work with top-notch graphic designers who can also develop logos and design items.

Website Audits

I can work with you to develop a website wireframe that aligns with your business branding and goals, or can audit a current website to consult how to improve your website's design, functionality, user experience and online conversion.

Digital Training

As well as consulting on how to improve your digital marketing, I can provide one-on-one or small group training. That way you can upkeep digital strategies in-house. Majority of training is in utilising e-communication systems, how to use and advertise through social media, how to understand and utilise website analytics.
